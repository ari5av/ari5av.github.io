The backtory of The Tour™ is incredible.

A couple of years ago, we moved my wife's grandma to a semi-assisted living facility, where she still lives.

She's a hoarder though, and she didn't want to lose any of her stuff. It's mainly clothes, handbags, shoes, costume jewelry, and some boxes of books nobody really wants.

We spent four months bagging up the clothes, building clothing racks in my basement, hauling it all over, and hanging it up, where it now remains.

She had used to be a professional dancer (think Broadway), so a lot of her stuff is kind of outlandish. A lot of The Tour™ has to do with the more ridiculous stuff I've found, but the most impressive part is the sheer quantity that's there.

Everybody's reaction to seeing my basement for the first time is different. One person literally broke down sobbing.
