Session Reports (latest first):

Session 16:

The party were surprised to discover that two of their previous companions, Niolas (human F1) and Hogan (dwarf F2) had been out adventuring on their own and were captured and enslaved by the very same orc party from the previous session, and were two of the redeemed slaves! Niolas and Hogan joined the party, and everyone got some well-needed rest.
DAYS 28 THROUGH 30
The party traveled (now afoot leading their remaining mounts) west-northwest along the Suss Road without incident, totaling approximately 60 miles.
DAY 31
The Suss Road began to gradually turn to the southwest, into the Suss Forest itself. The party made the decision to continue off the road into scrubland to the northwest, staying within sight of the coast of the Woolly Bay itself.
DAY 32
The party encountered a lone fire beetle which was dispatched with ease by several ranged weapons. Niolas harvested its fireglands, which will continue to glow as candlelight through day 35 but are currently stored in an opaque sack.
DAY 33
The party continued northwest through the coastal scrubland without incident.
DAY 34
Before dawn, while Bronan was on watch, a band of 12 goblins came upon the party. Glordir awoke immediately, and he and Bronan began to defend against the horde. During the second round, an elf appeared and joined the fray. The remainder of the party joined at the beginning of the fourth round, when most of the goblins were burned to a crisp by Clem's burning hands spell.
After the fight, the elf introduced himself as Killoriol (elf F1) and explained that he had been stalking the goblins for some time and attacked when he saw them attack an unwitting party of adventurers. Killoriol joined the party, end session.

Session 15:

END BASIC, BEGIN AD&D
The party made its way out of Cave G and back to the Keep safely, where several new members joined.
DAY 25
Durin (dwarf F3), Gabbai (human C2), Clem (human M3), Father Tuck (human C1), and faithful friend Bronan (P3) set forth from the Keep in a northeasterly direction toward the Suss Road, along with new companions Linus Padfoot (halfling T1), Glordir Woodkin (human R1), Groot (half-elf D1), Kaien (Mk1), and Gorgrim (half-orc F1). The goal was to reach the Free City of Greyhawk by taking the Suss Road north along the Wild Coast, then following the Selintan to Greyhawk itself.
After a few hours, the mounted party encountered several gnolls in the hills. The party gained surprise advantage, but the zealous Gorgrim became the unlucky target of one of the gnolls and was killed with one hit when a parley attempt failed. The remainder of the gnolls were dispatched with, and the clerics buried poor Gorgrim at the spot where he died. The party camped nearby for the night.
DAY 26
The day and night passed without incident as the party continued through the foothills of the Drachensgrab Mountains toward the north coast.
DAY 27
While passing in the valley between two hills, the party encountered a small animal of no description which was immediately pounced upon by a hungry maned lion from a nearby hill. Linus spotted the lion, and Groot carefully approached while casting speak with animals to assure the lion that the party meant him no harm and wished only to continue onward. The lion withdrew (with his kill), and the party reached the Suss Road, nearby which they pitched camp behind a berm.
Not long thereafter, Glordir spotted an oncoming party of armed orcs with two mean-looking wolves on leads, escorting four shackled slaves! Durin and Bronan ascended the berm, and Durin called out in orcish, indicating a wish to buy the slaves. After a tense encounter where Bronan was nearly slain or taken hostage, the slaves were ransomed for the price of 65gp and two horses, and the orcish party reluctantly moved on.
End session, the freed slaves have been brought into the party's camp, and the campfire has been re-kindled.

Session 14:

For once, the luck of the dice was with the party, showing no random encounters on the way back to the Keep, despite a slow pace due to the tied-up bandit prisoners. The prisoners were delivered into the hands of the Captain of the Guard and the party rested up
DAY 25
The Captain of the Watch asked the party to escort several watchmen to the designated outpost site
Niolas, Hogan, Marius, Drugmir, Durin, Clem, and Bronan set out with the watchmen; no random encounters, so the watchmen stopped off and started building the outpost while the party continued to the Caves of Chaos
The party decided to tackle Cave G at the far end of the valley, which was full of cracked bones and rotting corpses
It wasn't long before the party discovered that this was the lair of a vicious owlbear, which immediately ripped Marius limb from limb before a pack of seven giant rats joined the fight from behind, closing the party in a narrow cavern with seemingly no escape!
A somewhat useful sleep spell from Clem took out three of the rats, and a poisoned wheel of cheese thrown as bait took out two more, while the owlbear was slowly whittled down
One last room in Cave G held a pool with three gray oozes, and Hogan shot one with his crossbow several times until it collapsed into sludge, while the other two slid slowly into the water
End session, party remains in the room with the pool and the remaining two(?) gray oozes...

Session 13:

Back at the Keep, the party brought their broken friend Bronan immediately to the chapel for emergency healing by the Curate and his acolytes. In a last-ditch effort to secure divine help for Bronan, Clem even emptied his pockets into the donation box.
The party decided to rest and recuperate for a few days, advancing to...
DAY 24
Marius, Drugmir, Father Tuck, Gabbai, Niolas, Hogan, Durin, Clem, and Bronan met the Captain of the Watch in the Keep's tavern, upset that he found himself unable to protect law and order on the wilderness road
The party suggested the establishment of a watch outpost on the road, halfway between the Keep and the Caves of Chaos
The Captain agreed, but such a feat could not be accomplished while the Keep faced the constant threat of raiders and bandits gathering in the woods to the south
The party therefore headed south into the woods and came across a large clearing with a dozen evil men waiting for nightfall to raid the Keep and steal all of the horses stabled there! They dispatched the raiders, killing their captain and capturing their lieutenant and four spearmen to be dealt with by the authorities at the Keep

Session 12:

DAY 21
Marius, Drugmir, Durin, Gabbai, Niolas, and Hogan, all having healed up from previous attempts to fight the forces of Chaos, set forth upon their trusty mounts with their friend Bronan and new acquaintances, Lareth the Priest and his two silent acolytes, on the east road toward the Caves of Chaos
Unfortunately, the dice were not in their favor, and before the halfway point the party had encountered no fewer than three random encounters!
First was several giant oil beetles crossing the road, which were dispatched with ease
Second was a returning party of battered veteran adventurers, who gave some advice about one of the higher-up caves; it was mazelike and there were inexplicable sounds in the cave, seeming to come from all directions
Third, the party encountered a pair of hungry mountain lions which put up a very strong fight
Niolas used a flask of oil and torch to set one lion aflame, but the other savagely mauled Bronan before both ran off
The party asked their friend Lareth to cast cure light wounds on their injured friend, and Lareth realized he was caught, for he was in fact an Evil High Priest all along, and had instead prepared CAUSE light wounds! When he cast his spell, drawing negative energy and releasing it into his "patient", Bronan fell to the ground and writhed in agony while Lareth and his acolytes attacked the party
The acolytes were vanquished quickly, but Lareth's magical plate mail and magical shield proved difficult challenges for the party, who were quickly losing resources
During the fight, Lareth referred to someone he evidently serves, whom he calls "The Dark Lady"
Another burning flask of oil thrown at Lareth by Niolas was easily batted away, and as fate would have it, it fell directly onto poor Bronan, who now was at death's door and still on fire! Niolas quickly kicked dirt over him and smothered the flames with his own cloak
As the fire all around licked at the Evil High Priest's armor, he withdrew a scroll and read from it; before the final word of the spell, he announced to the party, "You haven't seen the last of me!" At the spell's climax, a loud bang was heard and a large cloud of smoke filled the area - when the smoke cleared, Lareth was gone!
End session, the party has returned to the Keep in much worse shape than previously thought
Gabbai advanced to level 2, and Durin advanced to level 3
